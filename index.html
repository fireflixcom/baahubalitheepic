Baahubali: The Epic brings together the complete saga of Mahishmati kingdom in one grand narrative. The story follows Shivudu, a young man with extraordinary strength who discovers his true identity as Mahendra Baahubali, the rightful heir to the throne. He learns of his father Amarendra Baahubali's legacy and his mother Devasena's imprisonment by the tyrannical king Bhallaladeva. With the help of the warrior Avantika and the loyal Kattappa, Shivudu embarks on a journey to reclaim his kingdom and avenge his father's death, culminating in a spectacular battle for the throne. This epic conclusion features breathtaking visuals, emotional depth, and the ultimate revelation of why Kattappa killed Baahubali, bringing closure to one of Indian cinema's most legendary stories.

FAQs

Why did Kattappa kill Baahubali?
Kattappa killed Baahubali because he was forced to obey the orders of Queen Sivagami, who was tricked by Bhallaladeva into believing that Baahubali was a traitor to the kingdom.
Is Baahubali The Epic a sequel or prequel?
Baahubali The Epic is neither a sequel nor a prequel. It is a special edition that combines both Baahubali: The Beginning and Baahubali: The Conclusion into one complete cinematic experience.
What makes Baahubali different from other Indian films?
Baahubali revolutionized Indian cinema with its grand scale, groundbreaking visual effects, epic storytelling, and record-breaking production values that were unprecedented in Indian film history.
